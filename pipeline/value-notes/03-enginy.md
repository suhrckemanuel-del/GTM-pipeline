# Value note — Enginy

**Vertical:** AI sales / outbound automation  
**Geo:** EU — Spain; expanding US, UK, DE, FR, IT, NL  
**Momentum cited:** €5M seed (Jan 2026); team growth; international expansion narrative.

## Observation

The category “AI SDR” is noisy. Buyers default to skepticism on deliverability, data quality, and who owns the pipeline when automation misfires. The companies that break through usually **anchor on one measurable wedge** (e.g. revive stale SQLs, book meetings from a defined TAM) rather than “replace your SDR.”

## Suggestion

Pick **one wedge + one geography** for a public 30-day experiment narrative (even with redacted metrics): same list, same offer, human vs AI-assisted sequence. Turn the output into a **benchmark-style asset** prospects can steal for internal business cases.

## Expected impact

Clearer differentiation vs generic AI outbound tools and more qualified demos from teams that already believe in the wedge you prove.
