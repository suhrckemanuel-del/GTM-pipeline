# Value note — Maisa AI

**Vertical:** AI / enterprise automation  
**Geo:** EU — Spain  
**Momentum cited:** ~$25M seed (Tech.eu 2025); scaling team and self-serve motion.

## Observation

“Enterprise workflow automation” is a **wide aperture**: RevOps, finance ops, and IT automation buyers all show up with different success metrics. When the hero story stays horizontal, outbound and demos attract a mix of low-intent testers and the pipeline gets noisy.

## Suggestion

Pick **one flagship vertical** for 6–8 weeks (e.g. finance operations or customer support handoffs) and make it explicit on the homepage hero, paid landing, and first case study: **named workflows, before/after time-to-complete, and who owns the bot**. Run a simple **ICP filter** on inbound (company size + system stack) before booking demos.

## Expected impact

Higher-quality pipeline and faster sales cycles because prospects self-identify—or disqualify—before the first call.
