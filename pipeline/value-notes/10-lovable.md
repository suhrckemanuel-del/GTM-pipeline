# Value note — Lovable

**Vertical:** AI / product creation (PLG)  
**Geo:** EU — Sweden  
**Momentum cited:** €14.6M+ seed (Sifted 2025); strong community + PLG motion; monetization and ICP focus.

## Observation

Viral PLG can scale usage faster than **pricing and packaging clarity**. Teams, agencies, and indie hackers often share the same top-of-funnel story, which makes it harder to tune upgrade triggers and sales-assist plays without feeling like you’re “taxing” the community.

## Suggestion

Run a **packaging experiment** on the upgrade path: separate “solo builder” vs “team shipping to customers” with one measurable outcome each (e.g. seats, projects, or publish workflows). Pair with a **cohort-based message** to power users (templates, collaboration, brand controls) instead of a one-size upgrade modal.

## Expected impact

Cleaner revenue per active user and a clearer story for when sales-assist should engage vs stay self-serve.
