# Value note — Nexus

**Vertical:** AI agents (enterprise)  
**Geo:** EU — Belgium  
**Momentum cited:** ~€3.7M seed (2026) with General Catalyst and YC; focus on production-ready agents and implementation support for non-technical teams.

## Observation

Enterprise “agent” buyers are fatigued by demos that feel like chatGPT skins. What wins early is **credibility in one messy workflow**: security, integrations, and measurable handoffs between human and agent. Your positioning already leans implementation-heavy — the GTM risk is sounding like a consulting shop unless productized proof is visible externally.

## Suggestion

Publish **one vertical-specific “agent in production” teardown** (anonymous customer ok): inputs, guardrails, KPI before/after, and time-to-live. Pair it with a narrow outbound list in that vertical only. This turns implementation strength into **repeatable marketing** instead of only sales stories.

## Expected impact

Higher reply quality from buyers who self-identify with the workflow, and shorter sales cycles because procurement sees a template for rollout.
