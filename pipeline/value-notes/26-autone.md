# Value note — autone

**Vertical:** Fintech — inventory / working capital  
**Geo:** EU — UK  
**Momentum cited:** Series A scale (~€15M+ in Sifted rankings context); inventory and cash flow positioning for product companies.

## Observation

Inventory-led finance products sit between CFO, ops, and merchandising. Messaging that is too “fintech” loses ops trust; messaging that is too “ops” loses CFO math. The GTM edge is **one measurable metric** (cash released, stock-outs reduced, PO cycle time) per vertical.

## Suggestion

Segment **retail vs wholesale vs hybrid** brands with different hero metrics and proof. Run a tight outbound experiment to 50 accounts per segment with segment-specific subject lines and a one-page ROI sketch. Compare meeting rate and sales cycle length.

## Expected impact

Clearer ICP focus and faster sales discovery because prospects feel the product was built for their operating model.
