# Value note — OpenFX

**Vertical:** Fintech / FX & liquidity  
**Geo:** US; expansion (e.g. SEA) cited in pipeline notes  
**Momentum cited:** ~$94M Series A (Mar 2026); volume growth; hiring + geographic expansion.

## Observation

FX and liquidity platforms win on **trust, depth, and corridor-specific behavior** as much as API ergonomics. When growth pushes into new regions, buyers ask different questions about **regulation, settlement, and local rails**—a single global “we move money” story can feel thin next to incumbents with local proof.

## Suggestion

For the next geography push, ship **corridor-specific pages** (route, use cases, compliance transparency, and what “good” latency looks like) plus one **implementation narrative** (onboarding steps, testing windows, support). Pair with a narrow outbound list: teams already shipping volume in that corridor.

## Expected impact

Higher intent conversations in new markets and fewer eval cycles lost to “we need someone who has done this here before.”
