# Value note — Kestra

**Vertical:** AI / workflow orchestration (open source + cloud)  
**Geo:** EU — France; expanding North America  
**Momentum cited:** €21M Series A (Mar 2026); Kestra 2.0 + managed cloud; explicit GTM expansion in NA/Europe; strong workflow volume growth cited in press.

## Observation

Orchestration is crowded (data, AI, infra). Buyers compare on reliability, observability, and time-to-first-successful-workflow. The risk in broad “unify everything” messaging is that **no team sees themselves** in the hero story.

## Suggestion

Pick **three enterprise workflow patterns** (e.g. incident response, finance close assist, customer onboarding) and ship interactive templates + before/after diagrams. Run AB tests on homepage and paid/organic landing pages to see which pattern pulls the most qualified demos in NA vs EU.

## Expected impact

Better conversion from evaluator to POC because prospects recognize a path that matches their org, not a generic orchestration promise.
