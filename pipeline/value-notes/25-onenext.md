# Value note — OneNext

**Vertical:** Fintech — B2B payments / embedded finance infra  
**Geo:** US  
**Momentum cited:** $5.5M seed (Jan 2025) led by Citi Ventures; roadmap includes international corridors and embedded lending; GTM expansion across US cities.

## Observation

Unified rails + compliance stories can read like a **feature checklist** (RTP, FedNow, ACH, KYB). Early customers need a **single hero outcome** — e.g. “cut time-to-first-payment for mid-market marketplaces” — or they bounce to best-of-breed point tools.

## Suggestion

Anchor GTM on **one corridor launch** (e.g. US → UK) with a named ICP (marketplace vs platform vs lender). Ship a landing + outbound sequence that only speaks to that ICP’s reconciliation and compliance pain. Expand only after reply data validates the wedge.

## Expected impact

Higher reply rates and cleaner product feedback because prospects match the first live corridor and use case.
