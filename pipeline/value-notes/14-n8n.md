# Value note — n8n

**Vertical:** AI / workflow automation  
**Geo:** EU — Germany  
**Momentum cited:** Series B scale; workflow automation with AI features; OSS roots + enterprise + self-serve.

## Observation

Strong OSS communities often stall commercial growth when the **upgrade path** feels fuzzy: self-hosted vs cloud, which seats matter, and what “enterprise” unlocks beyond hosting. Buyers compare you to Zapier/Make *and* to internal IT build-vs-buy—so the story needs to clarify **who n8n is for at which scale**.

## Suggestion

Ship **named migration and packaging stories** by segment: agencies (multi-client workspaces), mid-market IT (SSO, environments, approvals), and enterprise (audit, SLAs). Add a simple **decision guide** (“choose cloud if… choose self-host if…”) to reduce evaluation thrash.

## Expected impact

Higher conversion from free/active users to paid plans and shorter enterprise procurement because the commercial boundary is explicit.
