# Value note — Payabli

**Vertical:** Fintech — embedded payments / PayFac for software  
**Geo:** US — Miami (remote team)  
**Momentum cited:** $28M Series B (Jun 2025); $60M total raised; hiring across GTM, product, engineering.

## Observation

Embedded payments win when software companies see **revenue expansion** as a product feature, not a side integration. The competitive set is heavy; differentiation often comes from **vertical playbooks** (need-to-pay verticals, ISV segments) and co-marketing with platforms.

## Suggestion

Package a **“payments revenue launch kit”** for your top 3 ISV segments: email templates, one-pager for their end-customers, and a simple ROI worksheet. Arm one partner success or GTM person to run it with 5 design partners and log what blocks adoption (legal, underwriting, UX).

## Expected impact

Faster ISV launches and more repeatable partner-led growth instead of only direct enterprise sales.
