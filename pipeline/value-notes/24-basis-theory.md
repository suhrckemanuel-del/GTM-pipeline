# Value note — Basis Theory

**Vertical:** Fintech — payments data vault / agentic commerce  
**Geo:** US  
**Momentum cited:** $33M Series B (Oct 2025); explicit “agentic commerce” positioning; enterprise customers named in press.

## Observation

Agentic commerce sits at the intersection of **security, compliance, and product flexibility**. Buyers will ask how agents access credentials without creating new PCI or fraud surface area. The GTM win is to make policy + vaulting feel like **enabling speed**, not adding bureaucracy.

## Suggestion

Produce a **security narrative for agent workflows**: roles, token lifecycle, audit, and kill-switch — in language a VP Product understands. Pair with one “agent checkout” reference flow (Pinterest-style use case). Use this as the lead asset for enterprise outbound to product-led champions.

## Expected impact

Fewer security-driven stalls in POC and clearer internal routing to the right technical + risk stakeholders.
