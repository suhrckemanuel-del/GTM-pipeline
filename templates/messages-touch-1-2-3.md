# Outreach cadence (10–14 days)

Contact 2–3 people per company where it makes sense: founder/COO, Head of GTM or Growth, Head of People.

---

## Touch 1 — value first

**Goal:** One specific observation + one idea. Short. No resume wall.

Hi {{first_name}} —

{{one_line_context_on_their_company}}

{{specific_observation}}

{{one_suggestion}}

If any of this resonates, happy to share a tighter one-pager on {{angle}}.

Best,  
Manuel

---

## Touch 2 — add depth (3–5 days later)

**Goal:** Second concrete angle or mini breakdown; still concise.

Hi {{first_name}} —

Following up — one more thought on {{topic}}: {{second_idea_or_example}}.

{{optional_question}}

Best,  
Manuel

---

## Touch 3 — clear internship ask (3–5 days after touch 2)

**Goal:** Named project format, duration, why you.

Hi {{first_name}} —

Last note from me — I’m looking for a {{4–8}} week {{sprint_type}} internship where I can help with {{deliverable}}.

Relevant background: {{one_line_Gleef_VC_finance}}.

If there’s no formal program but a project like this would help, I’d love 15 minutes.

Best,  
Manuel

---

**Lightweight CTA (any touch):** “If useful, I can send a one-page GTM mini-audit for your {{segment/product}}.”
