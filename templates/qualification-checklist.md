# Qualification checklist (no points)

Use before writing a value note or sending outreach. All **must-haves** should be true.

## Must-haves

- [ ] **Momentum you can cite** — something concrete in the last ~6 months (funding, launch, hiring push, new segment, partnership, traction signal).
- [ ] **Reachable decision-makers** — 1–2 real people (founder, Head of GTM/Growth, Head of People) with a plausible channel (LinkedIn, email, intro).
- [ ] **Specific GTM hook** — one observable move or gap (positioning, ICP, channel, onboarding, launch narrative), not generic praise.
- [ ] **Internship-realistic org** — stage/size where a short founder-led project is plausible (avoid portal-only campus programs unless you have a warm angle).

## Nice-to-haves (break ties)

- [ ] **Pain matches your proof** — Gleef-style GTM, VC screening, or finance-heavy story fits.
- [ ] **Parallel contact paths** — founder + GTM + People without spamming.
- [ ] **Strong personal interest** — you would commit 8+ weeks genuinely curious.
- [ ] **Geo / language / timezone** — you can show up credibly on calls.

## Weekly throughput

Default ~**10 outbound targets/week** (e.g. 5 AI + 5 fintech). In a sprint you can raise volume only if every target still gets a **real** value note.
