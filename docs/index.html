05. Methodology

The intelligence framework behind every company in this pipeline.

01

ICP Definition

Map buyer archetypes across three tiers — enterprise, mid-market, and early-stage. Each tier gets its own persona, pain point, and qualification heuristic before any outreach begins. Momentum signals (funding, launches, hiring) must be within 6 months.

Trigger: momentum signal within 6 months 4 must-haves per company
02

Value Note Research

One specific GTM observation per company — not generic praise. Each note follows a structured hypothesis: Observation (what's happening) → Suggestion (one concrete experiment) → Expected Impact (measurable outcome). No message goes out without a real insight behind it.

11 value notes written across 50 companies Observation → Suggestion → Impact
03

Sequenced Outreach

A 3-touch cadence anchored on the value note hypothesis. Touch 1: context and insight. Touch 2: depth and specificity. Touch 3: the ask — a scoped 4–8 week internship sprint aligned to the identified GTM problem. Two routing groups based on company size.

2 batches · Group A: founders · Group B: GTM leads 3 touches · Day 1, 4, 8
